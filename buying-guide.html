Digital Food Scale Buying Guide

What really matters when choosing a kitchen scale — accuracy, capacity, display, and features that earn their keep.

See Top Picks →

1. Accuracy First

The most important spec is measurement precision. Most kitchen scales measure in 1-gram increments, which is fine for most cooking. Serious bakers and coffee enthusiasts should look for 0.1g precision scales. Check that the scale is accurate across its full capacity range — some budget models lose precision near their maximum weight.

2. Capacity Matters More Than You Think

An 11-lb (5 kg) scale handles most kitchen tasks. But if you bake large batches, make stocks, or weigh whole chickens, a 22-lb scale eliminates frustration. Consider how you'll use the scale in 2 years, not just today.

  • Casual cooks: 11 lb is plenty
  • Active bakers: 11–15 lb recommended
  • Large batch cooking: 22 lb gives you headroom

3. Display Readability

A scale you can't read quickly is a scale you'll stop using. Look for a large, backlit LCD or LED display. The OXO Good Grips' pull-out display is a brilliant solution for reading weights when a large bowl is on the platform. If the display isn't covered by your test bowl in the store, it's probably fine.

4. Platform Size and Material

Stainless steel platforms are more hygienic and durable than plastic. The platform should be large enough to hold a standard mixing bowl without tipping. Removable platforms make cleaning much easier — worth the small price premium.

5. Power and Battery Life

Most scales run on AAA or AA batteries. Some higher-end models offer USB-C charging — convenient but rarely essential. Look for models with auto-off features that preserve battery life. Battery scales are fine; just keep spares in the kitchen drawer.

The Bottom Line

Don't overthink it. A $25–35 scale from a reputable brand covers 90% of kitchen needs with excellent accuracy. Spend more only if you have a specific need — high precision coffee dosing, large capacity, or a nutrition app integration. Our top picks cover all these categories.
